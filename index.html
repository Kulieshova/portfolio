eyetech

Real-time animals' vision simulator

The web application EYETECH is a computer simulation of animal vision in real-time that can be used by students in zoology classes to make the learning process more exciting and visible.

This application allows you to view the world through the eyes of five animals: a dog, a snake, a horse, a cow, and a sparrow. Besides, students can read a quick summary of chosen animal's vision. And having mastered the material, students can test their knowledge on the topic of "Animal Vision" by taking a small test below on the page.
